About this project

The Mastercraft Bamboo Monitor Riser is a sturdy and stylish platform that elevates your screen to a more comfortable viewing heigt. Placing your monitor at eye level has the potential to improve your posture an make you make comfortable while at work, helping you stay focused on the task at hand

Featuring artisan craftsmanship, the simplicity of design creates extra desk space below your computer to allow notepads, pens, and USB sticks to be stored under the stand.
